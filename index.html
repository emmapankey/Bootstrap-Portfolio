About Me

Emma is a risk control specialist and the commercial account manager at an insurance agency in the New Hampshire seacoast area. Her success in this role is attributed to her ability to foster professional collaboration and her personal approach to client interactions both inside and outside the office. Emma is passionate about small business environmental policy and corporate environmental sustainability. In her current role she oversaw the conversion to a paperless office.

A New Hampshire native, Emma recently became a resident of Maine where she discovered a passion for setting up wildlife cameras and live cams in her yard and birdhouses. When she isn't chasing her daughter Layla and her dog Loki around you can find her in her garden.
